Imagine a world where education cost nothing to the student

Introducing SALAD (Students for Action and Learning Development)! SALAD is a revolutionary idea where students pay nothing out of pocket. The courses we provide are given, free of charge, thanks to generous sponsors who want students to succeed.
In addition to free education, students need to have school work on their own time. We at SALAD believe that school should happen at the students pace and not be rushed. When students are not rushed, they can succeed better and they can grow in their knowledge.
