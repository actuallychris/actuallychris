    URL: CNN.com

    Visitor Analysis

    Who is using the site?

    The typical person who will use CNN will be a male in his 30's-40's who has a bachelor's degree. He will typically have a basic knowledge of computers, and his income level is standard. Typically, they will be accessing the site on their laptop or on their cell phone.

    Persona

    John Brown is a person who regularly accesses CNN.com on his mobile phone as he is sitting down for his morning coffee before he goes to work. In addition, he is a person who is passionate about news and more specifically, he is passionate about his political views. Growing up in New York, he grew to love education and attended St. Johns University. After graduating in Buisness Management, he moved to Los Angeles, California, and now works for a multinational company. By most standards, he would be considered a liberal. With his views, he doesn't entirely agree with Donald Trump's policies, however, he does have respect for him. While browsing CNN, he typically will be searching for news about politics.
