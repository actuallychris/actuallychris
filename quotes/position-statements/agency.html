Agency

In the Book of Mormon, there are countless people that talk about agency. You have Nephi talk about it to Laman & Lemuel. Lehi taught his sons upon his deathbed, and Christ even taught about it in his sermons to the Nephites.
According to everything that the prophets and apostles have said, (as well as my personal opinion) we do NOT have "Free Agency." We do have agency, however, with that agency, we have concequences that goes with each of those choices. "Wherefore, men are free according to the flesh...and they are free to choose happiness and eternal life, or eternal damnation and endless misery and wo."
